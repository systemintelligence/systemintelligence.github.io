INTELLIGENCE

System Intelligence is an artificial intelligence research initiative that aims to build a future where advanced AI can design, implement, and operate next-generation computing systems.

While AI capabilities have advanced dramatically, key gaps remain to realize system intelligence. We must unlock AI's next stage of capabilities – reasoning about architectures and protocols, weighing tradeoffs, applying enduring principles, developing effective abstractions, and more – beyond coding and bug fixing. We believe that realizing system intelligence requires the collective efforts of the entire system research community and must be accomplished atop the decades of systems research expertise and experience (much of which today’s AI has never seen in its training data). Therefore, we're building system intelligence community and wants to leverage collective efforts to make this initiative move forward, making AI systems more widely understood, customizable and generally capable.

We are researchers, engineers, and builders across universities and industry labs, united by our passion for intelligent systems and their real-world realization. The team brings together expertise in both frontier systems research and artificial intelligence.

Community charter

Working on what matters most. We focus our energy on the problems that will have the greatest impact on the future of AI and systems research. By prioritizing meaningful challenges over easy wins, we ensure our work contributes to lasting progress.

A culture of respect and shared purpose. Our community is built on kindness, collaboration, and a commitment to help one another grow. We value diverse perspectives and treat each other with respect, even when opinions differ. Knowledge is shared openly, support is freely given, and everyone is aligned toward a common goal. In this environment, we lift each other up and advance together.
